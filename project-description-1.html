University Bus Management System

A safe and well maimtained transportation for all students.

About The Project

In today's fast-paced world, efficient transportation systems are crucial for the smooth functioning of any institution. Universities often face the challenge of managing transportation for students coming from various cities and areas. To address this need, we propose the development of a Bus Reservation System for our university. This system aims to streamline the process of booking seats on university buses, providing convenience and reliability to students.

The primary objective of this project is to create a user-friendly and efficient Bus Reservation System that caters to the transportation needs of our university's students. The system will allow students to register, log in, view bus schedules, and book seats on university buses. It will provide flexibility in managing transportation for both irregular transportation users and those who require daily transportation.
